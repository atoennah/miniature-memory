40 parts Ongoing You were born in shadow, the daughter of Gwi-Ma-the most feared demon in existence. But you turned your back on the darkness long ago, severing the blood tie that bound you to him and choosing to live among humans in silence and pain.
Now, you walk alone... until Huntrix finds you.
They're global idols by day, demon hunters by night-and you're the ghost in their shadow. The phantom fourth member who never steps on stage, but always shows up when the monsters come.
You're not made for the spotlight.
You're made for war.
