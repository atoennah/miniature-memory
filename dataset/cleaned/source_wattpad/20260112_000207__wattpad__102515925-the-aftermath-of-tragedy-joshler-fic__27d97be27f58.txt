addict in the clouds (joshler)
15 parts Complete There will be mentions of suicide and fighting involved in the story. They won't be too graphic, but if those do upset or trigger you, it would be recommended that you don't read this story.
On August 22nd, 2006, Tyler Joseph was found in the woods - and presumed dead.
But, only sort of.
He became an angel, sent to make sure his Hometown, Columbus, Ohio, was peaceful and tranquil.
But he didn't realize how much his death would impact everyone. His best friend Josh Dun, refuses to accept it, losing his sanity and his will to live when his best friend leaves him.
Because he wanted with all his heart for him and Tyler to be more serious than best friends.
(February 14th, 2017 - August 10th, 2017)
