53 parts Ongoing MatureBorn as mirror images but raised to be individuals, Arianna and Adrianna Knight are the twin daughters of CEO Adam Knight and artist Andrea Knight. Now stepping into their own stories, yet expected to follow in their parents' formidable footsteps, each sister must navigate the challenge of love and self-discovery.
Arianna is the living echo of her mother. Graceful, poised, and impeccably polite, she's all gentle smiles and quiet confidence. But behind the courteous veneer, she yearns for something more than inherited perfection, she craves a love that sees her, not her legacy.
Adrianna, on the other hand, is the family's darling of drama and expectation. Spoiled, outspoken, and magnetic, she thrives on attention, until the right person comes along and shows her how to care about someone else deeply. Will she be able to shed her bratty shield and open herself to real romance?
As both twins explore their own paths,Arianna in the art world, Adrianna in the high-stakes social scene, they discover that love doesn't come in a box marked "perfect." From stolen midnight confessions to unexpected heartfelt moments, they each must learn what it means to love authentically, beyond the shadow of their famous family name.
A heartfelt, dualperspective romance about two sisters learning to trust their hearts and one another.
