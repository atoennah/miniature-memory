Obsession Beyond Reason
66 parts Ongoing Book - 1 (Best friends series)
Aditya x Meera
Meet Mr. Aditya Singhania India's most feared mafia king.
Ruthless and merciless.
He's the enigmatic diamond tycoon, commands attention wherever he goes.
His chiseled jawline, piercing brown eyes and a cold, calculating demeanor mask his true identity.
Aditya rules his shadowy empire with a iron fist.
And there's Meera Sharma a short tempered and loving girl. Who want to live her life like all freedom.
But,
His Obsession is a wildfire, fierce and all consuming. In a world where power bent to his will, she is the one thing he cannot control, and that it drives him mad.
Her laughter haunted his sleepless nights, her defiance ignites a dangerous hunger in his veins.
He craves her like a man craves air, and no amount of chaos, bloodshed, or war can extinguish his need.
His world was built on fear and respect, but for her, he is willing to burn kingdoms to ash.
She is his addiction, his salvation, and his madness all wrapped in the one thing he was willing to ruin himself for.
