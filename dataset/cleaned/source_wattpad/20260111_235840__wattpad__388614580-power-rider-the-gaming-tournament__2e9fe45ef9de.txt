Kamen Rider System Birth of The Riders
10 parts Complete Video Games. Virtual Worlds that stimulate the mind and entertains the masses. At the center is the video game company Genm Industries. Genm holds multiple gaming tournaments yearly where pro-gamers compete, have fun and make money off pro-gaming.
A pro-gaming team comprised of three best friends are practically unbeatable and constantly win tournaments. After winning a recent tournament the three are chosen by Genm Industries CEO to fight a deadly virus that threatens the world as Kamen Riders!
