Son of Mischief (LokiAsgard Fanfiction - Selig's Story)
63 parts Complete Selig Lokison has spent most of his life being groomed to one day rule the Kingdom of Asgard. And although his parents have tried to make his upbringing as normal as possible, Selig is still very much his father's son and when your father is Loki, the God of Mischief, there's bound to be trouble.
Celia Baker has known Selig since they were children and she's the one person he could always count on. At least, he could until the one night he crossed the line with her.
But when Loki decides he's had enough of Selig's recklessness and banishes him to Midgard until he proves his worthiness, Selig has to convince Celia to help him get back home. Trouble is, not only hadn't he planned on losing his heart to her, but he also has no idea how to convince her that this time, he's not playing any tricks on her.
