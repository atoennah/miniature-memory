Sebastian Grae Miranda, a 17 years old 4th year highschool student.
Unlike other teenagers, Sebastian is an introvert kid who enjoys the company of his books. They say one's youth is an exciting roller coaster journey of experiences you must never miss.
With the arrival of some unexpected friends, Sebastian finally starts to break out of his shell and enjoy the sunshine of his teenage years.
By some misfortunate, or rather fortunate encounter, Sebastian gets to be closer with his unrequited childhood love. Will his love finally be reciprocated? Will he persevere despite all the obstacles? Will his last year as a highschool student be an unforgettable memory? Will it end in a "Happy Ever After" or in tragedy? Will his dream be reality or will he forever be stuck in a nightmare, unwilling to wake up from the falsehood and illusion?
