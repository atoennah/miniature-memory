She's The Daughter Of A God
45 parts Complete As punishment for the battle of New York, Loki moved into Avengers tower so he could learn how to change and how to be a good guy. But while Loki is at Avengers tower, he meets Tony Stark's adopted daughter, Leya. Loki and Leya become close. Loki goes behind Leya's back to find out about Leya's biological parents, only to find out that he is her father. Can Loki keep this a secret? How will he explain this to the Avengers? Should Loki trust them or should he keep this secret all to himself?
