Shapes of your memory (Michael Myers x Reader)
53 parts Complete Mature(Y/N) (L/N) was a regular 15 y/o high school student, not the most popular but neither hated, she just existed. One day she manages to befriend one of the prettiest and most popular girls in her class, Judith Myers.
Their friendship was odd and definitely not a sincere one, but (Y/N) couldn't care less because she found herself a new friend, Judith's young brother Michael.
At the time the girl meet Michael he was just 6, a small boy with blond messy hair and blue eyes, but the two of them instantly clicked and became inseparable. (Y/N) loved to take care of him, the boy's company was nice. Michael loved to spend time with the girl, it was fun and calm unlike in his house and she actually took care of him! Unlike Judith...
But then everything goes really, really wrong. (Y/N) had an accident and ended up in a hospital. When the girl returned she learned about what happened to the Myers family, to her friend...
What's the aftermath? What happened after 15 years since (Y/N) saw the little boy? Well, this is going to be a long story...
Most of the characters are created by me. The ones that lack of detailed description belong to the "Halloween" movie from 2007.
