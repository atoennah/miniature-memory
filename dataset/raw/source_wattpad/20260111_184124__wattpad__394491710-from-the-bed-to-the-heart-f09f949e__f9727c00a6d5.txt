22 parts Ongoing A new story begins... and this time, it's not just about love it's about everything that love hides.
Hidden Affection.
A tale where emotions run deep even when they're buried beneath anger, silence, and duty.
She's fierce, free spirited, and unpredictable a 24 year old with a heart full of spark, rebellion, and unapologetic dreams.
He's mature, composed, and everything she swore she'd never fall for a 28 year old man bound by rules, reputation, and silence.
They're nothing alike. She's chaos. He's calm. She questions everything. He follows structure. But sometimes, it's the opposites that burn the brightest when they collide.
When their worlds crash into each other, sparks fly but not the pretty kind. It's messy. It's intense. And beneath all that chaos... something begins to grow something they both try to deny.
A journey that starts with misunderstandings, cold stares, and unexpected encounters...
A story where hatred slowly softens into something unspoken. Where boundaries blur. And hearts, unknowingly, begin to lean in.
Tropes you'll find in this story:
• Arranged Marriage
• Professor x Student
• Chaotic Couple
• Slow Burn Romance
• Opposite attracts
This is not your typical romance.
It's about stolen glances, denial, unsaid feelings, broken rules and hidden care and the slow unfolding of affection that was never supposed to happen.
