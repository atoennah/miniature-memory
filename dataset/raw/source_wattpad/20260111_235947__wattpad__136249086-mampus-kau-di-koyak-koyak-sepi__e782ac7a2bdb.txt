**"A Love Written in Secret"**
What happens when love blooms in silence, hidden beneath layers of unspoken emotions?
A girl deeply rooted in her values, never imagined that a simple university function would change her life forever. Draped in her black abaya, hijab, and mask, she stood on stage, unaware that -the guest of honor-was watching her with admiration, mesmerized by her unwavering faith in a world that often questioned it.
From stolen glances at a lunch party to late-night conversations that became a habit, their connection deepened. Yet, neither confessed-until the day He was set to leave. Sitting alone on a university bench, lost in her thoughts, She never expected to see him kneeling before her, holding a bouquet of red roses.
*"Aaj kuch mat kehna... Bohat himmat kar ke yaha tak aya hoon. Pehle meri baat sun lo..."* His voice trembled, his heart in his throat.
Tears slipped from Her eyes as she finally heard the words she had longed for. In that one moment, in front of the entire university, their hearts spoke louder than society's judgment.
But love in secrecy comes with its trials. As they navigate the complexities of their hidden Nikah, insecurities arise. Will her fears push her away from him? Or will his unwavering devotion prove that true love never needs a witness-it only needs faith?
**A story of love, longing, and a bond tested by time.**
