Camilla came to Penbook college to start over. She is desperate to finally escape her past and carve out her own future, keep her father safe, and prove to herself she won't let her past define her.
The plan? Stay quiet, stay focused, and avoid anything that could complicate things. What could go wrong?
Then she met Gianni Mancini. Maddeningly attractive and dangerous in all the right ways, and hiding more than he lets on, Gianni was a factor Camilla didn't bank on, especially when he slept across the hallway from her and slept with anything that had two legs.
She learns too much too quickly and is sucked into a world she was trying to avoid. His world.
Faced with the temptation of funding for her father's care , Camilla makes a deal with the devil and agrees to keep an eye on Gianni. She's told to watch him, to get him back on track in his father's drug empire, but what happens when she get's too close?
Carefully-drawn lines begin to blur.
