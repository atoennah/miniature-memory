🔥 One reckless night. One forbidden secret. A love that could shatter an empire. 🔥
Amira Jacobs is picking up the pieces of her broken heart after catching her boyfriend cheating in their Brooklyn bed. Dragged to a glittering Manhattan rooftop club by her vibrant best friend Savannah, Amira's ready to drown her pain in cosmos and dance away the tears. But when Savannah stumbles out drunk, Amira's left alone-until she locks eyes with a dangerously sexy stranger in the VIP section. His smoldering gaze and wicked smirk promise escape, and one steamy, reckless night in a luxury penthouse leaves her breathless... and marked by his mysterious art-themed tattoo.
Weeks later, Amira's world flips: she's pregnant, and the stranger is no stranger-he's Mateo Rocco, billionaire CEO of a high-stakes auction house, ruthless mafia lord of an art smuggling empire, and Savannah's husband. Torn between guilt, desire, and fear, Amira's pulled into Mateo's dark, glamorous world of Manhattan penthouses, Brooklyn warehouses, and hidden speakeasies. As her baby bump grows, so do the secrets: Amira's own mafia-tied past, Savannah's heartbreaking betrayal, and a rival's deadly vendetta.
With passion igniting every forbidden touch, Amira must choose-run from the danger or fight for a love that could burn it all down. Will she protect her heart, her baby, and her future, or will Mateo's obsession claim them all?

After dropping out of university and setting her sights on traveling to Europe, twenty-three-year-old Tikera Copeland is desperate for a job. So when she innocently applies for a gardening position and immediately gets hired, she's shocked to discover that her "real" job will be tending to a bunker full of thriving, (definitely) illegal weed plants. Intrigued by this world she knows nothing about, Tikera agrees to stay, but her growing feelings for "The Boss" -- the mysterious drug kingpin who owns and grows the plants -- quickly complicate things. He's falling for her too (and she knows it, even if he won't admit it), but breaking through his icy exterior won't be easy. And with thieves, vengeful hitmen, and rival kingpins threatening them at every turn, will they even survive long enough to find their happily ever after?
