Breaking Boundaries (unordinary love story)
50 parts Complete Misty Gates - she's smart, nice, and a goody-two-shoes. Constantly treated as an outsider, she struggles to fit in.
Then there's Rick Hastings - a football jock, a bad boy, and a sadistic bully. He's dating the most popular girl in school, and does whatever he can to get what he wants.
So when Misty and Rick cross paths, it seems like they will never end up together. Misty has always hated Rick, or so she thought. Suddenly, things take a turn, and Misty can't help but wonder the strange feelings she feels around Rick. She feels an irresistible tug around him and wonders if she can resist him any longer, even if she doesn't wants to; but sometimes, in the right place at the right time, unordinary things happen. It's called love. And it will alter their lives forever. Misty lets Rick into her life, and her love completely transforms Rick, as they embrace their differences.
This story sheds light on the ups and downs of a relationship that sparks, and the heartbreaking things we will do for our other half. It is a story of love and discovery, forgiving and forgetting, laughter and tears, and simply moving forward. This story highlights the things that truly matter to us in our lives, the moments where we feel alone in the world but are not, and the true meaning of life. Most of all, it captures those bittersweet moments in true love and being a high schooler.
